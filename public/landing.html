IdeaGit

A design ideation tool. Select your assigned condition below to begin.

Condition 1
Manual Ideation
Create and develop design ideas through writing. Edit and finalize ideas at your own pace.
Condition 2
Structured Manual Ideation
Same as Condition 1, with a process tree on the left that tracks how each idea evolves over time.
Condition 3
AI-Assisted Ideation
Chat with AI to generate and develop your design ideas. Ideas and modifications are tracked automatically.
Condition 4
AI-Assisted Structured Ideation
Chat with AI to develop ideas while a full process tree shows how each idea evolved through your conversation.
